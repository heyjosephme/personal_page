Hi, I'm Joseph — a full-stack developer based in Tokyo. If you've found your way
here, welcome. This is the first post on a site I've wanted to have for a long time:
one place for the things I build, the things I read, and the things I'm figuring out
along the way.

## Who I am

I'm originally from China and now call Tokyo home. I work across the stack — mostly
Ruby on Rails 8 with Hotwire on the backend, and TypeScript with React and React
Native on the front. I like building small, useful products, shipping them, and then
writing about what I learned.

## What you'll find here

A few corners worth exploring:

- **[Projects](/projects)** — things I've built, like ThreeWeigh (a real-time Rails 8
  + Hotwire weight tracker), Keio Tsushin (a credit-management tool for
  distance-learning students), and Bumplink (a QR-based contact exchange app).
- **[Reading](/reading)** — the books I'm working through, with notes. I read to get
  better at the craft, and keeping a public list keeps me honest.
- **[About](/about)** — the longer story (plus a globe that measures how far you are
  from Tokyo).
- **[Now](/now)** and **[Uses](/uses)** — what I'm focused on at the moment, and the
  exact tools I reach for every day.

## What I'll write about

This blog is where I'll go into more depth than a project card allows:

- Technical deep-dives — Rails 8 + Hotwire, TypeScript and React patterns, the
  occasional infrastructure rabbit hole
- Indie-hacking progress: what I'm shipping, what's working, and what isn't
- Notes from dev life in Tokyo

I'd rather write a handful of honest posts than a pile of filler, so expect quality
over cadence.

## Even this site is a project

Because I can't help myself: this site runs on **Astro** with **React islands** for
the interactive bits, **Tailwind CSS** for styling, and it's deployed on **Cloudflare
Workers**. It's fast, it's mine, and I'll probably write about how it's put together
at some point too.

Thanks for stopping by. Have a look around — and if something resonates, come say hi.
